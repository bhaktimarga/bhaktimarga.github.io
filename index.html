Who We Are

A Living Tradition
for Modern Souls

Founded by Paramahamsa Vishwananda, Bhakti Marga is a global spiritual movement rooted in the ancient Vaishnava tradition. We offer a complete path — not merely a philosophy — that transforms how we live, love, and relate to the Divine.

With centres and communities across five continents, Bhakti Marga welcomes people of all backgrounds, religions, and walks of life. Our shared language is love; our shared destination is God.
