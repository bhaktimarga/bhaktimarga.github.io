Our Mission

Guiding Souls to
the Divine

Bhakti Marga — meaning "the Path of Devotion" — is a global spiritual movement dedicated to helping people of all backgrounds discover their own divine nature and cultivate a living relationship with God.

Founded by Paramahamsa Vishwananda in 2005 and rooted in the ancient Vaishnava tradition, Bhakti Marga weaves together timeless spiritual practices with the realities of modern life. We believe that devotion is not reserved for monasteries and ashrams — it is available to every human heart, in every moment.

Through yoga, meditation, scripture study, sacred rituals, kirtan, and the company of like-minded seekers, Bhakti Marga offers a living path: practical, joyful, and deeply transformative.

Paramahamsa Vishwananda

Spiritual Founder & Master

The Master

Paramahamsa
Vishwananda

Born in Mauritius in 1978, Paramahamsa Vishwananda displayed remarkable spiritual gifts from early childhood. Guided by an inner calling, he began teaching at age nineteen and founded Bhakti Marga in Germany in 2005.

His message is universal and simple: "Just Love." He teaches that unconditional love for God and all living beings is both the path and the destination — the means and the end of spiritual life.

Today, Paramahamsa Vishwananda travels the world, offering satsangs, retreats, initiations, and blessings to thousands of devotees. His presence at Shree Peetha Nilaya — the international ashram in Germany — is the spiritual heart of the global Bhakti Marga family.

Our Journey

Milestones on the Path

2005

Bhakti Marga Founded

Paramahamsa Vishwananda establishes the organisation in Springen, Germany, welcoming the first seekers to the path of devotion.

2008

Shree Peetha Nilaya Ashram

The international ashram becomes the spiritual home base — a sacred space for retreats, festivals, and deepening practice.

2012

Global Expansion

Bhakti Marga communities blossom across Europe, the Americas, Africa, and Asia, united by shared love and practice.

2016

Just Love Festival

The annual Just Love Festival is inaugurated, drawing thousands of pilgrims and seekers for days of immersive devotional celebration.

2020–Present

Bhakti Goes Digital

Online programmes, livestreams, and the BHAKTI+ platform extend the community's reach to every corner of the world, 24/7.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01
Universal Love

We believe every human being, regardless of background, religion, or caste, is equally worthy of divine love and spiritual growth.

02
Authentic Tradition

Our roots in the ancient Vaishnava lineage give depth and authenticity, connecting us to thousands of years of living wisdom.

03
Joyful Devotion

Spirituality should be alive, vibrant, and joyful — not a burden or performance, but a natural expression of love for the Divine.

04
Service & Giving

Seva (selfless service) is at the heart of our community — giving freely of time, energy, and resources as an act of devotion.

05
Living Practice

Spirituality is not theoretical. Daily meditation, prayer, yoga, and ritual transform philosophy into lived experience.

06
Global Community

From Germany to South Africa to the Americas, we are one family — diverse in culture, united in the language of the heart.

A global spiritual movement founded on love, devotion, and the timeless teachings of Paramahamsa Vishwananda.
